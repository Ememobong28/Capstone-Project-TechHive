# Capstone-Project-TechHive

**Description**

TechHive is a specialized website for college students in the tech field, offering a streamlined platform to search for internships, discover conferences, and access programs for enhancing their experiences. It aims to solve the problems of overwhelming internship searches by categorizing opportunities based on academic year and providing a supportive community through integration with ColorStack. By connecting students with peers who have completed relevant internships, TechHive empowers them to make informed decisions and pursue successful tech careers.

**Category:** [Education/Professional Development]


**Story:** [TechHive is all about helping college students in the tech field find the right internships and enhance their experiences. Students can sign up, create a profile, and easily search for internships categorized by their academic year. They can also explore relevant conferences and programs. The unique aspect is the integration with the ColorStack community, where students can connect with peers who have completed internships and gain valuable advice. TechHive aims to simplify the internship search process, provide access to educational opportunities, and foster a supportive network for students pursuing tech careers.]


**Market:** [TechHive is primarily designed for undergraduates in the tech field, providing them with a platform to simplify their internship search and enhance their experiences.]


**Habit:** [The frequency of app usage for TechHive would vary depending on the individual student's needs and goals, but it is anticipated that users would engage with the app regularly, potentially on a weekly or bi-weekly basis, to search for internships, explore conferences, access programs, and interact with the ColorStack community for advice and support.]


**Scope:** [The initial scope of the TechHive app is to provide a comprehensive platform for college students in the tech field to search for internships, discover conferences, access programs, and connect with the ColorStack community for guidance. The primary focus is on internship opportunities and facilitating interactions within the tech community.]

**User Stories**


**User Roles**

**Student:** A college student in the tech field who is actively seeking internship opportunities to gain practical experience and enhance their career prospects.


**User Personas**

**Employer/Company Persona:**
Name: John


Company:  Pinterest


Motivation: Post new Internship openings and find talented college students for internships at their tech company.



**Student Persona 1:**


Name: Emily


Location: San Francisco, USA


Age: 19


Technology Access: Proficient with laptops and frequently uses the website for internship searches.


Motivation: Emily is a sophomore computer science student looking for a summer internship related to software development. She wants to find internships specifically tailored to her academic year and gather insights from other students who have completed internships in the tech field.

**Student Persona 2:**

Name: Alex


Location: New York City, USA


Age: 21


Technology Access: Comfortable with smartphones and uses the mobile app to access internship information on the go.
Motivation: Alex is a senior computer engineering student searching for a final-year internship that aligns with his interests in hardware design. He wants to explore internship opportunities and benefit from the advice and experiences of other students who have completed internships.

**User Stories**


**Required:**
As a student, I want to create a profile, so that I can showcase my skills and academic year to potential employers.


As a student, I want to search for internships based on my academic year, so that I can find opportunities that match my current level of study.


As a student, I want to view internship details, including company information and application instructions, so that I can make informed decisions about applying.


As a student, I want to connect with other students who have completed internships, so that I can ask questions, seek advice, and learn from their experiences, potentially allowing students to chat with each other.


As an employer, I want to post internship opportunities, to attract college students to apply for internship positions at our tech company.
As an employer, I want to be able to chat with students and answer their questions.


**Stretched/Optional:**


As a student, I want to explore conferences I can attend to grow my network in the tech industry.


As a student, I want to discover programs I can take part in to learn more about tech companies and also develop my skills


As an employer, I want to browse conferences and programs on the platform, so that I can identify opportunities to engage with students and promote our tech company.
